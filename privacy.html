Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2, 2026

Privacy First: ClarityTimer is designed with your privacy in mind. All your session data and custom breathing patterns are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to ClarityTimer ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our breathing and meditation timer application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

The App allows you to create and store data locally on your device, including:

  • Custom breathing patterns
  • Breathing pattern configurations (inhale, hold, exhale durations)
  • Meditation timer settings
  • Session completion history
  • App preferences and settings (theme, language, notifications)

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information
  • Contact information (email, phone number, name)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Health or wellness data

3. How We Use Your Information

All data created in the App is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is used solely to:

  • Display your custom breathing patterns
  • Track your meditation and breathing session history
  • Remember your app preferences and settings
  • Provide app functionality

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure, encrypted storage mechanisms provided by your device's operating system. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

4.2 Export Functionality

The App includes optional export features that allow you to:

  • Create a backup of your session history and custom patterns in JSON format
  • Save the backup to your device or share it via standard sharing mechanisms
  • Import previously exported data

When you export data, it is your responsibility to ensure the security of the exported file.

4.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data, including:

  • Secure local storage using platform-provided encryption
  • No transmission of data over networks
  • No third-party access to your data

5. Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • Data persists on your device until you delete it
  • You can delete individual session history entries
  • You can clear all session history
  • You can delete custom breathing patterns
  • Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Audio and Notifications

The App uses the following device features:

  • Audio Playback: For meditation bells and audio cues (processed locally)
  • Haptic Feedback: For breathing rhythm cues (processed locally)
  • Notifications: For session interval alerts (local notifications only)
  • Screen Wake Lock: To keep your screen on during sessions (optional)

6.2 No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used our App, please contact us.

8. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, there are no international data transfer concerns.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification of material changes

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, you have complete control:

  • Right to Access: All your data is accessible within the App
  • Right to Export: Use the export feature to create a copy of your data
  • Right to Delete: Delete data directly within the App or by uninstalling
  • Right to Portability: Export your data in JSON format for use elsewhere

11. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since we do not collect, sell, or share personal information, these rights are not applicable to our App.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with GDPR principles:

  • Data is processed lawfully and transparently
  • Data is collected for legitimate purposes (app functionality)
  • Data is kept secure on your device
  • You have full control over your data
